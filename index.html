About Me

Im a 4th year Human Centered Computing student at Rochester Institute of Technology. I had a short stint as a Computer Science student, but I was frequently more concerned with how someone was using a system rather than the system itself.

My interests in UX are currently focused on the field of AR and VR. It excites me that user interactions and experiences are no longer limited to a flat screen, but now focus on more spatial experiences.

I am a student ambassador for my majors department, where I bring student feedback directly to department heads. I am also the President of the RIT UX Club where I head club meetings.
